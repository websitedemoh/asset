Overview

Sri Asset Plus ARC is an Asset Reconstruction Company (ARC) focusing in all sectors across the country.

It aims to help genuine borrowers by restructuring loans and offering an increased time for settlement. Where possible, it strives to assist honest borrowers settle accounts. However, when dealing with recalcitrant borrowers, the company acts according to the statutory provisions and spares no effort in recovering due sums.

Together we make it possible

The team at Sri Asset Plus ARC is a group of individuals eager to take up new challenges and completely commit themselves to every project. Comprising of a healthy mix of youthful vigour and rich experience, the team members share a common belief of taking Sri Asset Plus ARC to greater heights or new peaks.

Learn, Enjoy and Grow

Recognising potential, nurturing it and imparting learning: Sri Asset Plus ARC offers excellent opportunities for growing and becoming a formidable professional. And it's not just about work, at Sri Asset Plus ARC you're assured of a healthy work-life balance as well as an environment that makes work exciting and fun.
