About Us

About Us

Sri Asset Plus ARC is an Asset Reconstruction Company focused on asset resolution and recovery across various sectors throughout India. The company works with a balanced approach, aiming to support genuine borrowers through practical restructuring solutions, extended settlement opportunities, and fair resolution methods wherever possible.

At Sri Asset Plus ARC, we believe that every financial case requires careful understanding, professional handling, and responsible action. Our objective is to help honest borrowers settle their accounts in a structured and transparent manner. At the same time, in cases involving unwilling or non-cooperative borrowers, the company proceeds firmly in accordance with applicable statutory provisions and takes all necessary steps to recover outstanding dues.

Together We Make It Possible

Sri Asset Plus ARC is driven by a committed and capable team that is ready to take on new challenges with dedication and discipline. The team brings together youthful energy, professional commitment, and valuable experience, creating a strong foundation for effective asset resolution and long-term growth.

Under the leadership of Mr. Kishan Sharma, Director, and Ms. Guddi Sharma, CEO, the company continues to move forward with a clear vision, ethical practices, and a strong focus on achieving meaningful results.

Learn, Enjoy and Grow

Sri Asset Plus ARC believes in recognising potential, nurturing talent, and encouraging continuous learning. The company provides an environment where professionals can grow, develop their skills, and become strong contributors in the financial and asset reconstruction sector.

Along with professional growth, Sri Asset Plus ARC also values a healthy work-life balance and promotes a workplace culture that is positive, motivating, and engaging. The company believes that when people learn, enjoy, and grow together, stronger results become possible.
